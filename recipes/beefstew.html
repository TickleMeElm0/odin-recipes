Colombia beef Stew (Sudado de Res)

Sudado de Carne is a traditional Colombian beef stew. This dish is the perfect one pot meal served over white rice, a light salad and avocado on the side. It's a stew made with beef, chicken, pork, seafood or meatballs simmered in a flavorful broth with potatoes, yuca, spices, fresh herbs and vegetables like bell pepper, carrots, garlic and onions. There are many variations of this dish in Colombia and different recipes depending in the region and the cook.

Ingredients

  • Chuck roast
  • Canola or vegetable oil
  • Onion
  • Scallions
  • Red bell pepper
  • Garlic
  • Tomato
  • Potatoes
  • Yuca
